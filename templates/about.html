Who We Are

Built by Community,
For Africa

EYT Gaming is a multi-pillar esports ecosystem based in Lagos, Nigeria. What began as a small group of passionate competitors has grown into one of West Africa's most complete esports infrastructures — operating as a competitive team, tournament organiser, training institution, broadcast hub, and grassroots community platform, all under one unified mission.

At the centre of our organisation is the EYT Dojo — a solar-powered esports training facility at 119 Erelu Ayisat Oniru Road, Maroko, Lagos. Players who travel from far distances are provided accommodation and meals, creating a stable learning environment free from economic pressure.
