Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EYTGaming collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform. By using EYTGaming, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: username, email address, password (hashed), display name
  • Profile data: bio, avatar, country, city, date of birth, phone number
  • Gaming identifiers: Discord username, Steam ID, Twitch username, in-game names
  • Tournament and match history, team memberships, coaching session records
  • Payment information (processed securely via Paystack — we do not store card details)
  • Communications you send us via support or contact forms

We also collect information automatically when you use the platform:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
  • Device information: operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage data: features used, tournament registrations, session activity

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and profile
  • Process tournament registrations and coaching bookings
  • Process payments and send transaction confirmations
  • Display leaderboards, rankings, and public profiles (subject to your privacy settings)
  • Send notifications about tournaments, sessions, and platform updates
  • Improve platform features and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Tournament organizers — to manage events you register for
  • Coaches — limited profile info when you book a session
  • Payment processors — Paystack processes payments; we share only what is necessary
  • Service providers — hosting, email delivery, analytics (under data processing agreements)
  • Law enforcement — when required by law or to protect rights and safety

Public profile information (username, display name, game stats, tournament results) is visible to other users unless you set your profile to private in your privacy settings.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account. Some data may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution purposes.

5. Your Rights & Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data (Settings → Export Data)
  • Correct inaccurate profile information (Settings → Edit Profile)
  • Control profile visibility (Settings → Privacy)
  • Opt out of marketing emails (Settings → Notifications)
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication where available.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and analyse platform usage. Essential cookies are required for the platform to function. You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

EYTGaming is not directed at children under 13. Users aged 13–17 require parental consent to register. If you believe a minor has registered without consent, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email or an in-platform notification. Continued use of EYTGaming after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

Contact our privacy team at privacy@eytgaming.com or visit our About page for more information.
